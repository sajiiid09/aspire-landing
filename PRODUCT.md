# Aspire Global Education

## Product

Aspire Global Education is a public marketing site for an international education consultancy. It helps prospective students and their families understand Aspire's services, explore study destinations, and move into a counselor conversation, course search, or the external student portal. It also explains the sub-agent partner offer for B2B inquiries.

## Users

- Prospective international students comparing courses, destinations, and application support.
- Parents and family members evaluating the credibility and clarity of the service.
- Education sub-agents considering an operational partnership with Aspire.

## Brand register

The experience should feel assured, cosmopolitan, and methodical. It is premium through restraint, clarity, and calm confidence rather than decorative luxury. The Cinematic Navy visual system, international-campus photography, and concise editorial composition are the core brand signals.

## Experience principles

- Make high-stakes education decisions feel understandable and well supported.
- Lead with verified scale and factual operating detail; never invent proof, partnerships, or outcomes.
- Keep calls to action consistent: Talk to a Counselor, Explore Courses, Student Portal, and Partner With Us.
- Use a professional institutional type system with strong readability and measured hierarchy.
- Preserve generous spacing, purposeful imagery, and restrained motion.

## Anti-references

- Multi-theme demonstrations or visual gimmicks.
- Neon, cyberpunk, AI-purple, or generic startup styling.
- Template-like consultancy card grids without a clear editorial hierarchy.
- Fashion-editorial typography that makes practical information feel ornamental.
- Fabricated testimonials, institutional logos, success rates, milestones, or certainty.

## Accessibility and constraints

- Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard operation, visible focus, logical headings, and clear form labels and status messages.
- Respect reduced-motion and reduced-transparency preferences.
- Remain a fully static Next.js export with no backend, API routes, authentication, CMS, or runtime image optimization.
