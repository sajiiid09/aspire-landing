## **Business Requirement Document (BRD) Addendum: Web Content, Asset Guide & System Architecture** 

**Project Name:** Aspire Global Education Official Website **Target Audience:** B2C Students & B2B Sub-Agents **Line Spacing:** 1.15 

## **1. Goal of the Website** 

The core objective of the Aspire Global Education website is to serve as a high-conversion, premium digital storefront that establishes unshakeable trust with prospective students and B2B sub-agents. It bridges the gap between massive global educational infrastructure and localized, expert consulting. 

- **Lead Generation:** To capture high-intent inquiries from students wishing to study abroad. 

- ● **Brand Authority:** To project the scale of an enterprise operation (750+ universities, 75K+ courses) while providing a white-labeled, proprietary technology feel. 

- **Operational Hub:** To function as a gateway where students and sub-agents log into a secure system to manage applications seamlessly. 

## **2. What It Does & How It Works** 

The platform operates on a dual-layer architectural model balancing public marketing with a private processing engine: 

## **What It Does:** 

- **Course Exploration:** Allows users to scan and filter through over 75,000 international programs across destinations like the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, and an extensive network of European nations. 

- **Document & Application Management:** Facilitates end-to-end processing from profile evaluation and SOP reviews to final visa submissions. 

- **B2B Sub-Agent Onboarding:** Empowers downstream recruitment partners to submit student profiles directly under the Aspire Global umbrella. 

## **How It Works:** 

1. **The Public Layer (Front-End):** Built on WordPress with Elementor Pro for maximum speed and beautiful UI presentation. A user lands on the page, reviews the statistics, and engages with a lead capture form or clicks the "Global Course Finder" button. 

2. **The White-Labeled Layer (Middleware API/Portal):** When a user logs in or runs a deep search, they are routed to a secure portal gateway. All third-party software identifiers, logos, and origin domains are dynamically stripped and replaced with Aspire Global Education branding. 

3. **The Backend Engine:** Applications are funneled through specialized workflows (Admissions, Compliance, Visa) overseen by internal counselors to guarantee a marketleading visa success ratio. 

## **3. Global Study Destinations** 

Aspire Global Education operates on a truly global scale. We offer direct access to elite educational institutions in major global tech hubs and robust European academic centers: 

- **Tier-1 English Speaking Destinations:** United Kingdom (UK), United States of America (USA), Australia, Canada, New Zealand (NZ). 

- **Schengen & European Union (EU) Destinations:** Germany, Sweden, Spain, Greece, Cyprus, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, and other emerging European academic hubs.

## **5. Professional Photo & Imagery Briefing Asset** 

To maintain a premium corporate look, avoid low-quality stock images. Use the following visual conceptual guide for sourcing photography: 

- **Hero Background Image:** A crisp, wide-angle lifestyle shot of modern university campuses combining historic architecture with glass-paneled innovation labs. Alternatively, use a shot of a diverse group of confident students walking through an international campus quadrant, shot in soft natural morning light. 

- **Destination Grid Visuals:** High-resolution, iconic yet minimalist cityscapes or landmark elements with subtle overlays. (e.g., London with a clean view of the Westminster Palace framing an educational setting; Canada featuring a modern Toronto skyline; Germany featuring clean Berlin architectures or futuristic learning labs). 

- **Service Section Icons:** Uniform, thin-line vector icons styled in your primary corporate color palette (e.g., Deep Navy Blue `#1A2B4C` or Polished Gold `#C5A059`). Do not mix colored 3D icons with flat 2D variants.
